About Joe

I'm not here to sell you a system.
I'm here to help you fix what's broken.

I'm Joe McAdam. I'm based in Pittsburgh, and I've spent most of my working life in operations and sales — watching good businesses slowly fall apart because they grew faster than their processes did.

Not because the owner wasn't working hard. Usually the opposite.

The problem I kept seeing was the same one: everything was in someone's head, in someone's inbox, or on someone's to-do list. Nothing was built to run without them. So when things got busy, things got missed. When things got missed, money left.

I started building systems to fix that — simple ones, things that actually work in the real world — and eventually turned that into the work I do now.

I'm not a consultant who tells you what to do and leaves. I build the thing, hand it off working, and show your team how to run it.

Where Things Break

Two kinds of broken.
Both cost money.

Most of the owners I work with aren't failing. They're just leaking — time, leads, energy, clarity. Here's what that usually looks like.

In your life

  • You're constantly in reactive mode — no margin, no clarity, no room to think
  • Your priorities feel scattered and you can't tell what actually matters right now
  • You're working harder than you should be for the results you're getting
  • You feel behind, but you can't identify where you fell behind

In your business

  • Leads come in and go cold before anyone follows up
  • Your intake process depends on the right person being available
  • You can't tell at a glance where your pipeline stands
  • You're doing $20/hour tasks because no one else is doing them
  • If you stepped away for two weeks, things would break

These aren't character flaws. They're structural problems. And structural problems have structural solutions.

How I Work
